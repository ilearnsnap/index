NATURAL AND SYNTHETIC MATERIALS AND FABRICS

What kind of fodder is given to sheep?

Sheep graze on grass and leaves

They are given pulses, corn, oil cakes, jowar and minerals

During winter they are provided grass, leaves, dry fodder and grains, indoors

In which season are sheep usually sheared?

Sheep are sheared in hot summer season

FORESTS, PLANTS, REARING AND AGRICULTURE

What are leguminous crops?

Leguminous crops have root nodules containing nitrogen fixing bacteria

What do you mean by leaving the land fallow?

Land is left untouched and uncultivated

List some traditional hand-held farming tools.

Sickle, hoe, shovel, pruning saw, khurpi, plough, axe, rake

What do you mean by fodder?

Food given to domestic herbivores like cow, goat, sheep etc.
